We are the tenth group, and today we will introduce the progress of our group’s physical items. Each person will report on the part they are responsible for
李I am responsible for the design of excavator walking device and the realization of excavator rotation function. At present, the basic 3D modeling design has been completed. The walking device of the excavator is roughly divided into three parts, the first part is the excavator rocker arm, the second part is the excavator driving wheel, and the third part is the excavator track.
The first is the excavator’s rocker arm. The rocker arm is installed under the chassis of the excavator, and the rocker arm device is driven by the steering engine, so as to realize the 360 degree rotation of the excavator. It allows excavators to dig in all directions for more efficient excavation. On the right is a 3D model of the rocker arm.
The second is the driving wheel part of the excavator. The gear part of the excavator walking device is divided into four wheels, which are: drive wheel, guide wheel, support wheel and sprocket. The most important of these is the drive wheel, which can be driven by an electric motor to make the excavator walk. The image on the right is a 3D modeling of the main drive wheel.
Finally, the track part of the excavator can increase the contact area between the excavator and the ground, thereby reducing the pressure. As shown in the picture on the right, the 3D modeling of the excavator track.
Finally, the three-dimensional modeling diagram of the whole excavator walking device is given. The three-dimensional modeling diagram of the excavator’s overall walking device shows the assembly relationship and the overall structure of each component.
At present, the physical model is still being purchased and assembled. This phase requires precise manufacturing and assembly in strict accordance with the 3D modeling diagram, ensuring that each part is the right size and position. At the same time, it is also necessary to carry out quality testing on key components to ensure that they can meet the actual needs of use. This diagram shows a mock-up of the current purchased chassis, along with some detailed parameters. The excavator chassis is made of aluminum alloy, and the track is made of engineering plastic. Tracks made of engineering plastic are lightweight and durable, and can adapt to a variety of difficult terrain and harsh environments.

孙我负责的部分是挖掘机中的动臂大臂及其移动。为了确保动臂可以承受预期的工作重量并且减少实物制作工作的困难，我们选择了如下结构。能够确保动臂的支撑效果，且便于3D打印制作。
我们要是动臂完成抬升以及旋转两个动作。抬升动作通过电机转动控制钢丝绳缩短或伸长，使动臂竖直方面的抬升。旋转动作由位于底盘的转动架和电机完成。
这是我们模拟的最终运动效果图。
The part I am responsible for is the boom arm and its movement in the excavator. In order to ensure that the boom can bear the expected working weight and reduce the difficulties of the physical production work, we chose the following structure. Can ensure the support effect of the boom, and easy to 3D printing production.
If we complete the lifting and rotation of the boom. The lifting action shortens or extends the wire rope through the motor rotation, so that the vertical lifting of the boom. The rotation action is completed by the rotating frame and the motor located in the chassis.
This is the final motion of our simulation.

杜 我负责的推压机构部分，已经完成模型优化的细节部分，购买了电机和一些所需的零件，已经对部分零部件进行了3D打印。1第一个部分传动轴，已经完成了打印，齿轮的配合良好，可以进行装配。2第二个部分电机我选择并购买了一分钟15转的电机。3第三个部分连接齿条部分，我们在3D打印最初的模型后发现斗杆的强度不够，最初的模型在安装的过程中断裂，于是我对模型进行了优化，将斗杆与齿条进行连接，增加其受力面积。暂时没有打印，后续可能考虑找金属3D打印。4第四个部分抱轴滑动块部分，起初我打印完它之后发现斗杆与它过盈配合了，导致滑块在斗杆上不能平滑地滑动，我将孔径增加了0.8毫米后打印地滑块可以使用。
The pushing mechanism part that I am responsible for has completed the details of model optimization, purchased the motor and some required parts, and has already 3D printed some of the components.
The first part of the transmission shaft has been printed and the gear fit is good, ready for assembly.
I chose and purchased a motor with 15 revolutions per minute for the second part.
The third part is connected to the rack section. After 3D printing the initial model, we found that the strength of the bucket rod was not enough. The initial model broke during installation, so I optimized the model by connecting the bucket rod to the rack to increase its stress area. There is currently no printing available, and we may consider finding metal 3D printing in the future.
The fourth part is the sliding block part of the shaft. At first, after I finished printing it, I found that the rod had an interference fit with it, which caused the slider to not slide smoothly on the rod. After increasing the aperture by 0.8 millimeters, the printed slider can be used.

雷The setting of the servo code was completed last semester, so we are currently improving the code for driving the motor and controlling it using SPI communication protocol. SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) is a commonly used serial communication protocol used for high-speed, full duplex data transmission between microcontrollers, sensors, and peripheral devices. Through the SPI communication protocol, fast data exchange between devices can be achieved, including the transmission of control signals and sensing data.
In terms of controlling motors, the SPI communication protocol is also widely used. Through the SPI interface, precise control of the motor controller can be achieved, including real-time adjustment of parameters such as motor speed, direction, and acceleration. Meanwhile, SPI communication also has the characteristics of high-speed transmission, simple wiring, and good stability, making it suitable for scenarios with strict requirements for motor control.
The use of SPI communication protocol to control motors can improve the response speed and accuracy of the system, while simplifying hardware design and debugging processes. Therefore, as one of the interfaces for controlling motors, the SPI communication protocol has been widely used in industrial automation, robotics, unmanned vehicles, and other fields.

白
我对单边履带用simapps进行了受力分析，后续我将对挖掘机行走轨迹进行仿真。总的来说我们组完成了部分零部件的3D 打印和电机和国标小零件的采购，将于下周内完成组装并进行试验调试。I conducted a force analysis on a single track using Simaps, and in the future, I will simulate the walking trajectory of the excavator. Overall, our team has completed the 3D printing of some components and the procurement of motors and national standard small parts. We will complete the assembly and conduct testing and debugging within next week. That’s the end. Thank you all for listening.
